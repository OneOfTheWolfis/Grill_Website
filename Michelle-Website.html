About me

Welcome to my personal website!

I’m Michelle Grill, a 20-year-old creative enthusiast born and raised in the beautiful city of Vienna.

My passion for the digital arts led me to study at HTL Spengergasse, where I specialized in Game Design.

From a young age, I’ve always been captivated by the power of storytelling and visual expression.

This fascination has driven me to develop a diverse skill set that includes drawing, a bit of coding, video editing, photographing, as well as 3D modeling and animating.

Each hobby allows me to explore new dimensions of creativity and bring my imaginative ideas to life.

I invite you to explore my portfolio, where you’ll find a collection of my projects showcasing my journey through the dynamic world of game design and digital art.

Divine Bliss

Divine Bliss is a 3D card game that tells the story of Sachi, the Goddess of Happiness.

Join Sachi as she descends to Earth, determined to spread joy and bliss among the people.

Through strategic gameplay, players help Sachi fulfill her divine mission, bringing happiness to all.

Insanity

Insanity is a 3D horror game developed as a school project in 2023.

The game immerses players in the story of a young protagonist trapped in a maze.

As they navigate the twisting corridors, they must protect their sanity by clinging to the fragile light of their candle.
